About Pak Garage

Pak Garage is transforming the way used-cars are traded in Pakistan. Car owners can now easily sell their cars through our secure, convenient, and hassle-free process. Those who wish to sell their cars and dont know how much to sell them for in the current market can use our service to get an close approximation for the selling price of their vehicle or if someone wishes to by a used vehicle and wants to determine if the dealer is trying to scam him out of money he can use our service to compare how much the vehicle should cost and the price at which it is being sold.
